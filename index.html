Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Full-featured chat with the freedom of self-hosting. No compromises.

Self-Hosted Servers

One docker compose up and you're live. PostgreSQL or SQLite — your data stays on your hardware. Every feature, zero cost.

Seamless Traversal

One client, unlimited servers. Jump between self-hosted and official servers without missing a beat. Your friends list and DMs follow you everywhere.

Voice & Video

Crystal-clear WebRTC powered by mediasoup SFU. Up to 25 users per channel with adaptive bitrate and bundled TURN for NAT traversal.

Permissions & Roles

Granular bitfield permissions with role hierarchy, channel overrides, and a full audit log. Familiar if you've used Discord — but you own the rules.

Architecture

Client-Hub Federation

Your client is the hub. It connects to independent servers while a lightweight central service handles identity, friends, and DMs.

Get Started

Deploy in Seconds

Spin up your own server with Docker. Full features, no license fees, no limits.

docker-compose.yml
version: '3.8'
services:
  ecto:
    image: ecto-chat/ecto-server
    ports:
      - "3000:3000"
    volumes:
      - ecto-data:/data
    restart: unless-stopped

volumes:
  ecto-data:

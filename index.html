Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Full-featured chat with the freedom of self-hosting. No compromises.

Self-Hosted Servers

One docker compose up and you're live. PostgreSQL or SQLite — your data stays on your hardware. Every feature, zero cost.

Seamless Traversal

One client, unlimited servers. Jump between self-hosted and official servers without missing a beat. Your friends list and DMs follow you everywhere.

Voice & Video

Crystal-clear voice and video powered by WebRTC and mediasoup SFU. Up to 25 users per channel with adaptive bitrate and bundled TURN for NAT traversal.

Permissions & Roles

Granular bitfield permissions with role hierarchy, channel overrides, and a full audit log. Familiar if you've used Discord — but you control the rules.

Architecture

Client-Hub Federation

Your client is the hub. It connects to any number of independent servers while a lightweight central service handles identity, friends, and DMs across all of them.

Get Started

Deploy in Seconds

Spin up your own Ecto server with Docker. Full features, no license fees, no user limits. Your server, your rules.

docker-compose.yml 
version: '3.8'
services:
  ecto:
    image: ecto-chat/ecto-server
    ports:
      - "3000:3000"
    volumes:
      - ecto-data:/data
    restart: unless-stopped

volumes:
  ecto-data:
